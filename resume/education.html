Educational Experience

As a teacher, I value and respect edcation. The items below highlight outlets I've used to expand my understanding of the world around me, grown professionally, and attempted to satisfy my quest for understanding.


  1. Metro Community College

    2. This institution has provided me with a well-rounded approach to IT principles. I have taken courses relating to networking, network security, digital forensics, Java programming, XML programming, and have had a broad introduction to the career fields that are available within the tech field. I have participated in a blend of educational methods from inclass, hybrid and online.

  2. University of Nebraska at Lincoln

    3. In participating in courses at UNL's graduate program, I embodied the skills of a competent and confident professional.     4. I engaged in classes, field experiences and projects to obtain a Bachelors Degree in Education, Physical Education.     5. I graduated from Creighton Preperatorty High School in 2003. Creighton Prep is an all male preperatorty school, known for its excellence in education and its promotion of "men for others". I had an opportunity of being involved in athletics, volunteering events, and social clubs.
