Educational Experience

As a teacher, I value and respect edcation. The items below highlight outlets I've used to expand my understanding of the world around me, grown professionally, and attempted to satisfy my quest for understanding.


  1. University of Nebraska at Lincoln

    2. In participating in courses at UNL's graduate program, I embodied the skills of a competent and confident professional.
